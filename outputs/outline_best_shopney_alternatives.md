## 2. Tapcart: The "Standard" Widget Builder [~200 words]
- **Positioning**: The biggest name in the space. Famous for their block-based builder.
- **Pros**: Easy to use, lots of widget integrations, good drag-and-drop.
- **Cons**: Rigid templates (look like everyone else), very expensive at scale (Enterprise plans), "Usage-based" limits on push sometimes.
- **Link**: [Tapcart Alternative](https://www.mobiloud.com/comparisons/tapcart-alternative) (Deep dive comparison).
- **Choose if**: You want a standardized, "app-like" look and have a simple catalog.

## 3. Plobal Apps: The Mid-Market Contender [~150 words]
- **Positioning**: Solid customized apps for Shopify Plus brands.
- **Pros**: Good integration with tools like Yotpo/Klaviyo, dedicated success managers.
- **Cons**: Still template-based at the core. Pricing can get high with success fees.
- **Choose if**: You need hands-on help but want a block-based builder.

## 4. Vajro: The Budget-Friendly Entry Point [~150 words]
- **Positioning**: Great for smaller stores or those just starting.
- **Pros**: Affordable, quick setup, good live selling features.
- **Cons**: Limited design flexibility, "cookie-cutter" feel, support availability.
- **Choose if**: You are budget-conscious and need an app fast (live selling focus).

## 5. Custom Agency Development: The "Blank Slate" Option [~150 words]
- **Positioning**: Hiring an agency or building in-house.
- **Pros**: Infinite possibility. You own the code.
- **Cons**: **Cost** ($50k+ upfront), **Time** (6+ months), **Maintenance** (you are the IT team now).
- **Link**: [Native App vs WebView App](https://www.mobiloud.com/blog/native-app-vs-webview-app) (Why custom often isn't needed).
- **Choose if**: You are Nike/Amazon and need AR/VR or non-commerce features.
