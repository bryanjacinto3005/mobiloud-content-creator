## 1. Executive Summary

### Context
Mobile apps are now one of the most powerful revenue and retention channels for ecommerce brands. This report aggregates data from MobiLoud customers and industry benchmarks to demonstrate the ROI of mobile apps.

### Key Takeaways
*   **Revenue Drivers:** Apps contribute **10–30% of total online revenue** for many brands, and up to **60%** for top performers.
*   **Superior Metrics:** Apps outperform mobile web with **3.5–7x higher ARPU**, **1.7–3x higher conversion rates**, and **10–50% higher AOV**.
*   **Retention Engine:** App users have **2.8–5x higher LTV**. Push notifications (especially abandoned cart flows) generate significant revenue with zero ad spend.
*   **High ROI:** Brands using MobiLoud often achieve **100x+ ROI** in a single quarter.

## 3. App vs. Mobile Web Performance

Most brands have a mobile site. The app's role is to **maximize value from top customers**, not replace the site.

*   **Conversion:** 9.1% (App) vs 1.1% (Web) → **8.0x Lift**
*   **Purchaser Rate:** 25.9% (App) vs 1.2% (Web) → **21.5x Lift**

#### Brand B – Luxury Fashion
*   **ARPU:** $39.63 (App) vs $1.45 (Web) → **27.3x Lift**

*   **Conversion:** 2.8% (App) vs 1.5% (Web) → **1.9x Lift**

## 4. Traffic Volume & User Behavior

A small app audience drives massive value.
*   **Brand A:** 16% of traffic → 65% of mobile revenue.

App users treat shopping apps like social media (frequent, long sessions).
*   **Session Duration:** ~6.5 minutes (App) vs <2 minutes (Web).

## 8. Industry Trends (2025)

### Consumer Adoption
*   **76.5%** of US smartphone users use shopping apps.
*   **64%** prefer apps over mobile sites.
*   **Demographics:** 61% of millennials have retail apps; 58% prefer purchasing via app.

### In-Store Integration
*   **74%** of global consumers use retail apps while shopping in physical stores (price checks, coupons).

## 10. Conversion Impact (Industry-Wide)

*   **Conversion Rate:** Apps convert **1.7x better** than mobile web (~3.5% vs 2.0%).
*   **AOV:** Apps see **10–50% higher Average Order Value**.
*   **Cart Recovery:** Apps recover **17% more** abandoned carts than email/SMS alone.

## 11. Retention & Lifetime Value (LTV)

*   **Retention:** Apps increase retention by **up to 5x**.
*   **Repeat Purchase:** 60% of first-time app buyers purchase again.
*   **LTV:** App customers have **2.8–5x higher LTV** than web-only customers.

---

## 12. Push Notification Strategy

### Best Practices
*   **Visibility:** Push has ~2x the visibility of email.
*   **CTR:** 3–5% (3–5x higher than email).
*   **Timing:** Weekends and late afternoons perform best (2.7x more purchases).
*   **Frequency:**
    *   Fashion/Beauty: 2–5 per week.
    *   Home/Furniture: ~1 per week.

---

## 13. Growth Strategies: Getting Installs

The key is converting *existing* web traffic into app users.
*   **CPI:** ~$2.50–$4.50 (but often $0 if converting owned traffic).
*   **Tactics:** Smart banners, pop-ups ("10% off first app order").
*   **App Store Conversion:** ~30% of page views convert to install.

## 14. Future Outlook
*   **Privacy:** Apps enable first-party data collection in a post-cookie world.
*   **AI & AR:** Apps are the natural home for AR try-ons and AI personalization.
*   **Social Commerce:** Apps mimic the "endless scroll" of social feeds, driving discovery.
